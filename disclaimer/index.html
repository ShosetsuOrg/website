Shosetsu

Free and open source novel reader for Android.

# Disclaimer

Shosetsu does not, in any way, own copyright over any of the novels present in its catalogs or extensions.

All DMCA requests should be forwarded to the original source, as Shosetsu does not host anything.

Shosetsu is a highly specialized web browser, utilizing external or third-party addons to provide information to display to the user.
